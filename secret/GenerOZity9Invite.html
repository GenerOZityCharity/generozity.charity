Ahoy!

GenerOZity #9 is running at the end of April for 48 hours, from midday on the 30th of April to 2nd of May. This is a formal invite asking if you’d like to come join around 50 other Aussie and New Zealand content creators collaboratively working together to do some good Down Under.

If you haven’t heard of GenerOZity, we’re an Australian livestream charity marathon that brings together streamers, YouTubers, community groups, eSports orgs, and podcasts for one non-stop live broadcast, with all money raised going straight to the charity we’ve chosen for the show.

Last year for GenerOZity #7 we raised just over $69,000 for bushfire relief to help deal with the aftermath of the Australian bushfires and for GenerOZity #8 we raised $25,000 for Cure Cancer at the PAX Online event.

This show, we’re supporting Cure Cancer again as they are an incredible cause that directs funds to researchers with the aim of making us the last generation to ever have anyone die of cancer.

Physical Segments:

The event is based in Sydney in a mansion of a house just down the road from Chatswood shops. You’ll be able to kick back before or after your set right near a variety of amazing restaurants, but also hang out inside a home with other people doing their segments. It’s a fun time.

Remote Segments:

If you can’t make it or don’t feel comfortable moving around during COVID, we have an amazing and simple way to have you start streaming on your channel (same as you usually do) and we then pull that feed into our channel as well.

The Address:

33 Tulip St, Chatswood, 2067

It’s only a few minutes from Chatswood station, right near both the train station and the bus depot of Chatswood.

If you’re after a cheap flight, JetStar can usually get you from Melbourne to Sydney for about $60-80.

If you’re looking to crash at the house after your segment, let us know! It’s a very big house and we’ve been preparing with a variety of beds, mattresses, blow up mattresses and comfy couches.

Other invites:

If you want to bring a friend, let us know ahead of time, we’d love to have them; we’ve got plenty of room to hang out. If you can think of any other content creators or community groups that you’d like to invite that might be interested, let us know!

P.S: Tweeting about the event is fine, but please don’t give out the location publicly.

Hope to see you at the show!
